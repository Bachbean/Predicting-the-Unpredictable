# Predicting-the-Unpredictable
## Objective:
In classical physics, the motion of objects can often be predicted precisely using laws
such as those formulated by Newton. However, many natural systems exhibit inherent uncertainty,
making long-term prediction extremely difficult. A particularly fascinating class of such systems is
known as chaotic systems, which arise in fields ranging from astronomy to quantum physics and
atmospheric science. In this project, you will learn what chaos is, how it emerges from deterministic
rules, and why it limits predictability. You will also explore its implications for weather forecasting
and climate modeling.
